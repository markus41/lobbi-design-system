Certification Levels

Recognized credentials that validate skill, experience, and commitment to quality

🏆 Master Craftsman 🎖️ Journeyman Artisan 🎓 Apprentice ✓ Verified Maker 🌱 Sustainable Practice ⭐ Featured Artist

Master Craftsman

Level 3

The highest level of certification. Requires 10+ years of documented experience, portfolio review by master panel, creation of masterwork piece, and mentorship of at least two apprentices.

  • 500+ hours documented practice
  • Masterwork submission & defense
  • Mentorship requirement
  • Peer review evaluation

Journeyman Artisan

Level 2

Intermediate certification for established makers. Demonstrates proficiency in core techniques, consistent quality, and professional practice standards.

  • 200+ hours documented practice
  • Portfolio of 10 completed works
  • Peer evaluation
  • Business practices course

Apprentice Certification

Level 1

Entry-level certification for emerging makers. Validates foundational skills and commitment to craft excellence. Perfect for those starting their journey.

  • Complete 3 foundational courses
  • 50+ hours practice minimum
  • Submit 3 portfolio pieces
  • Skills assessment
