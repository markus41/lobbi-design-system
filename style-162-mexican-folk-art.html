Featured Collections

Talavera Pottery

Creative

Discover authentic hand-painted Talavera ceramics from Puebla, featuring traditional cobalt blue patterns and vibrant floral motifs passed down through generations of master artisans.

Alebrije Sculptures

Hospitality

Vibrant wooden sculptures hand-carved and painted with fantastical designs. Each alebrije is a unique piece of art representing the creativity and imagination of Oaxacan craftspeople.

Papel Picado

Media

Delicate cut-paper banners featuring intricate designs celebrating life's special moments. Traditional papel picado brings festive energy and cultural heritage to any celebration.
