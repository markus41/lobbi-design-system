Featured Heritage Site

Church of the Intercession on the Nerl

A masterpiece of medieval Russian architecture, this white-stone church was built in 1165. Its elegant proportions and harmonious design exemplify the golden age of Vladimir-Suzdal architecture.

Location: Vladimir Oblast, Russia
Period: 12th Century
Status: UNESCO Protected
