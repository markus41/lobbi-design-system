Style 81: Teachers Union + Education Advocacy

Design Blend

80% Teachers Union (solidarity, collective action, labor rights) + 20% Education Advocacy (policy reform, public engagement)

Temperature

6/10 - Warm & Supportive: Approachable yet authoritative, balancing professional formality with member solidarity and support

Formality

7/10 - Professional Union Standards: Clear hierarchies, official documentation, contract language, grievance protocols

Color Psychology

Education Red (#b91c1c): Solidarity, action, urgency | Academic Navy (#1e3a5f): Knowledge, authority, trust | Apple Green (#65a30d): Growth, achievement, renewal

Typography Strategy

Headings: Source Sans Pro (sans-serif) - clear authority, professional clarity | Body: Merriweather (serif) - readable, scholarly, trustworthy

Target Audience

Education unions, teacher associations, faculty organizations, K-12 and higher ed members seeking representation, benefits, and professional advocacy

Key Design Elements

Contract status tracking, grievance management, PD credit system, legislative alerts, member benefits, retirement planning, chapter directories

Unique Differentiators

Collective bargaining visualization, solidarity indicators, action item deadlines, member engagement metrics, union density tracking, contract expiration alerts
