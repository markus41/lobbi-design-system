Curriculum & Courses

MUS 401

Advanced Piano Performance

In-depth study of advanced solo repertoire with emphasis on interpretation, technique, and stage presence. Includes jury performance.
Instructor: Dr. Victoria Moreau
4 Credits
MUS 315

Chamber Music Ensemble

Collaborative study and performance of chamber music repertoire from Baroque through contemporary periods. Weekly coaching sessions.
Instructor: Prof. James Wellington
3 Credits
MUS 250

Performance Practice Seminar

Historical approaches to performance including ornamentation, articulation, and stylistic interpretation across major musical periods.
Instructor: Dr. Elizabeth Chen
3 Credits
MUS 490

Senior Recital Preparation

Intensive preparation for senior recital including program selection, technical refinement, and performance psychology. Required for all seniors.
Instructor: Applied Faculty
2 Credits
