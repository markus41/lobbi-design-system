Skill Certification Programs

Earn recognized credentials in traditional crafts and artisan skills

Apprentice Craftsman

Level 1

Complete 3 foundational courses and demonstrate basic proficiency in your chosen craft discipline.

Journeyman Artisan

Level 2

Complete 6 intermediate courses, 100+ hours of practice, and create 3 portfolio pieces for evaluation.

Master Craftsman

Level 3

Complete 10+ advanced courses, 500+ hours documented, create masterwork piece, and mentor 2 apprentices.

Sustainable Practices

Specialty

Complete coursework in eco-friendly materials, zero-waste techniques, and sustainable sourcing practices.

Design Innovation

Specialty

Demonstrate ability to blend traditional techniques with contemporary design. Portfolio of 5 innovative pieces required.

Craft Educator

Specialty

Journeyman level required. Complete teaching methodology course and successfully lead 3 workshops or classes.
