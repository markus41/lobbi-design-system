Climate Change Mitigation through Oceanic Carbon Capture

Prof. Sarah Thompson, FRS • Environmental Sciences

This groundbreaking research demonstrates a novel approach to carbon sequestration utilizing naturally occurring oceanic processes. Our findings suggest potential reduction of atmospheric CO₂ by 15% within two decades through scalable implementation.

FRS High Impact Peer Reviewed

Quantum Computing Applications in Cryptography

Sir Michael Davies, FRS • Theoretical Physics

Development of quantum-resistant cryptographic protocols essential for future information security. This work establishes new mathematical frameworks for post-quantum cryptographic systems, ensuring data security in the quantum computing era.
