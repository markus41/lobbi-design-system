Status: Committee Review | Last Updated: Dec 5, 2025

Proposed legislation to simplify business tax filing and reduce compliance burden for small businesses. Chamber submitted testimony supporting amendments that protect local businesses. Next hearing scheduled for Dec 19.

SB-1903: Workforce Development Initiative

Status: Passed Senate | Last Updated: Dec 1, 2025

$50M investment in regional workforce training programs with focus on advanced manufacturing and technology sectors. Chamber advocacy helped secure $8M for apprenticeship programs.
