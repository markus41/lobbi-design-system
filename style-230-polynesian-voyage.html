Featured Voyage

Hōkūleʻa World Tour

Join the legendary double-hulled voyaging canoe Hōkūleʻa on a historic journey connecting Pacific islands using traditional wayfinding techniques—no modern instruments, only stars, waves, and ancestral knowledge.

Duration: 47 days
Route: Hawaiʻi → Tahiti → Rarotonga → Samoa
Departure: March 2026
Crew: Traditional Navigation

Traditional Navigation Techniques

Star Compass

Navigate using 32 points around the horizon, marked by rising and setting positions of key stars.

Wave Patterns

Read ocean swells and wave reflections to detect land masses beyond the visible horizon.

Bird Navigation

Observe seabirds' flight patterns and species to determine proximity and direction to land.
