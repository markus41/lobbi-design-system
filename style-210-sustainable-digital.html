Sustainable Design Principles

Energy Efficient

Dark Mode First

Default dark theme reduces screen energy consumption by up to 60% on OLED displays, significantly lowering the carbon footprint of digital interactions.

Impact: High Savings: 60%
Minimal Resources

Single Font Family

Using only Atkinson Hyperlegible with system font fallbacks eliminates unnecessary HTTP requests and reduces data transfer while maximizing accessibility.

Requests: 1 Fallback: 0 KB
Accessible

WCAG AAA Compliant

High contrast ratios (7:1 minimum), readable typography, and semantic HTML ensure accessibility for all users, reducing the digital divide.

Standard: AAA Contrast: 7:1+
